Food Culture

Food Culture is a mobile application designed to help food enthusiasts desiring expand their palette and discover different cultural food through the tap of a screen.

Overview

My team and I were tasked to design a mobile application that solves a conflict that users experience in their everyday lives. We decided to design a food app that helps people seek and try different cultural food while providing a user-friendly experience.

UX/UI designer throughout the whole design process, particularly in the following areas:

3 Weeks

User Research

To begin our research, we designed our research questions with the intention of knowing the characteristics, behaviors, and pain points of our users. We conducted five zoom interviews with people who utilize different tools and methods to discover different types of cultural food. To gather more data, we also created a survey on Google.

Through our research questions, my team and I discovered a trend of individuals willing to try different kinds of cultural food; however, they have a hard time deciding exactly what and where to eat.

The Problem

People have difficulty searching and ordering different cultural food and restaurants in their area.

They want to view genuine reviews and recommendations to help them choose where and what to eat.

The Solution

Design a "one-stop" shop where people can search for specific cuisine and restaurants, view reviews from other users, and order directly from restaurants of their choice.

Definition & Ideation

In order to determine what features we should prioritize for our mobile application, we created a feature prioritization matrix. From our research interviews and data, we brainstormed features that focused on our user pain points. After, we categorized our ideas based on their complexity and potential impact.

Through this exercise, we concluded that the most important features to consider were:

  • In-depth review system
  • Detailed filters
  • A "surprise Me" feature-- a randomizer that gives users personalized recommendations
  • Menu interfaces
